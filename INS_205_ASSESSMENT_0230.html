About This Page

This webpage was developed as part of the INS205 — Application Design and Development course assessment at the University of Jos.

What I find most compelling about web development is how much a well-crafted page can communicate — not just information, but personality, care, and intentionality. A thoughtfully designed interface says something about the person who built it.

This project applies HTML5, CSS3, and basic JavaScript to create a structured, styled, and interactive personal webpage. Through it, I have developed a clearer understanding of semantic document structure, visual hierarchy, responsive layout, and how JavaScript can interact with the DOM at runtime.

Going forward, I hope to deepen my skills in both design and development — building digital experiences that are accessible, visually refined, and genuinely useful to the people who use them.
